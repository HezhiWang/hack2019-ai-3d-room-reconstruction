State-of-the-art technologies

Our pipelines leverage state-of-the-art technologies for 3D reconstructions. From a video we extract the sharpest images and use structure-from-motion to construct a sparse point cloud from the images. Next we perform a process called "cloud densification" to turn the sparse point cloud into a dense point cloud, before triangulating the dense point cloud into surfaces. Finally, the surfaces are textured over by overlaying images, and the resulting object mosaic is packaged into a 3D object for your viewing pleasure.
